Project Intro

Our project goal is to create a rideable holonomic mobility platform. This means that each wheel can spin around the horizontal axis while pivoting around the vertical axis. This has the effect of making any mobility platform highly maneuverable. Imagine having an electric skateboard, but rather than making wide turns, you can turn at any rate you like.
